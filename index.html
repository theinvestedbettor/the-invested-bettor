FAQ

The honest answers.

Is this gambling advice? Are you guaranteeing wins?

No. Every pick is a hypothetical, model-based edge — never a guarantee. Past performance does not indicate future results. Sports betting carries real financial risk. We publish receipts for every play (wins and losses) so you can evaluate the process, not chase outcomes. This is not financial advice.

How are picks graded? Can I trust the ROI numbers?

Every pick is recorded before games begin and graded against publicly reported final scores using the line we captured at the time of the recommendation. The master tracker is updated daily by an automated process — no cherry-picking, no hindsight. Losing days are recorded the same as winning ones. Nothing is hidden.

What sports do you cover?

NBA, NHL, MLB, NFL, NCAA Basketball, NCAA Football, and Formula 1 (race days only). Daily picks are filtered to the highest-edge spots — typically 2 to 5 plays per day, never more.

Why "unit-based" instead of dollar amounts?

Units scale with your bankroll. We use a hypothetical $1,000 bankroll where 1 unit = $10, but the same percentages apply whether your bankroll is $500, $5,000, or $50,000. Sizing rules: High confidence = 2u max, Medium = 1u, Low = 0.5u. We recommend most readers cap daily exposure at 5% of bankroll for sustainable bankroll management.

What if I just want to try it before paying?

Free tier is forever-free. You will get one preview pick per day, the weekly recap PDF, and full access to the public tracker. No credit card required. If the numbers prove themselves, upgrade. If not, stay free.

Is gambling responsible? What if I have a problem?

Never bet more than you can afford to lose. Sports betting can be addictive. If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, call 1-800-GAMBLER or visit ncpgambling.org. We will never encourage chasing losses or any behavior that prioritizes recovery over discipline.
